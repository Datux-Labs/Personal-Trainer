60-Minute Strength Blueprint (Mon/Wed/Fri)

1) Mobility Primer (10 min)

  • World's Greatest Stretch — 5 reps/side
  • 90/90 Hip Switches — 10 reps total
  • Single-Leg Deadlift (bodyweight) — 10 reps/side

2) Functional Compounds (25 min)

  • Goblet Squats — 3×12
  • Romanian Deadlifts (RDLs) — 3×12
  • Renegade Rows — 3×10/side

3) Stability & Prehab Circuit (15 min)

  • Lateral Lunges — 2×12
  • Pallof Press — 2×12
  • Farmer's Carry — 3×45 sec

4) Flexibility Cool-Down (10 min)

  • Couch Stretch — 1 min/side
  • Pigeon Pose — 1 min/side

Guidelines for Sustainable Execution

  • Easy Mandate: Keep morning runs/swims at recovery intensity (Zone 2) so evening sessions stay high quality.
  • Saturday Pivot: Alternate long hike and long run to build endurance and running durability.
  • Sleep Requirement: Prioritize 7–9 hours/night for adaptation and recovery.
  • Listen to Niggles: If ankle/shoulder tightness persists, replace a run with an easy swim or 30-minute foam rolling session.
